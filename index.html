About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to help nurses and other healthcare professionals globally who would like a chance to come and work in Denmark. As of today, there is a need of 1000 nurses in Denmark, and we are committed to fulfilling that need.

Our Values

Our Expertise

Authorisation for Non EU Healthcare Professionals

We provide strategic legal advice to our clients and offer processing of their applications as well as recruitment. We also process work permits for clients wishing to work whilst waiting for their authorisation.

Authorisation for EU Nationals

We provide legal assistance including processing of applications to healthcare professionals who are EU citizens or have been trained in the EU.

Accompanying Family Member

Authorisation applicants have the privilege to bring their families with them. In order for our clients have a hassle-free experience during the authorisation process we also extend our services to their families. We provide legal counselling and processing of applications for residence permits for accompanying family members as well as help find free public schools for your kids.

Work Permit

As part of the authorisation process is learning the language, authorisation applicants are allowed work part-time whilst learning Danish. We provide assistance in processing of work permit applications.
